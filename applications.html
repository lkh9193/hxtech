Microscopy & Advanced Imaging

High-performance imaging solutions for life science and materials research, from routine microscopy to advanced in vivo and volumetric imaging.

Typical Applications

  • Live-cell and time-lapse imaging
  • Super-resolution and sub-cellular imaging
  • Light-sheet and 3D volumetric imaging
  • Intravital and in vivo microscopy
  • Neuroscience and developmental biology
  • Materials and surface imaging

HX Technology works closely with researchers to design imaging workflows that balance resolution, speed, phototoxicity, and long-term stability.

Spectroscopy & Optical Analysis

Reliable spectroscopy and optical measurement tools for accurate characterization of materials, samples, and optical systems.

Typical Applications

  • Absorbance, transmission, and reflectance measurements
  • Fluorescence and lifetime-based analysis
  • Optical material characterization
  • Quality control and process monitoring
  • Teaching and research laboratory experiments

Our solutions are designed to be flexible, scalable, and suitable for both standalone measurements and integration into larger experimental setups.

Laser & Photonics Applications

Precision laser and photonics solutions supporting advanced research, imaging excitation, and micro- and nano-scale fabrication.

Typical Applications

  • Fluorescence and nonlinear microscopy excitation
  • Two-photon and multi-photon imaging
  • Micro- and nano-fabrication
  • Photonic device research and testing
  • Optical system development and OEM integration

HX Technology assists customers in selecting laser sources that meet performance, safety, and integration requirements, ensuring reliable operation within complex optical systems.

Our Application-Driven Approach

Every application comes with unique technical requirements. HX Technology begins each engagement by understanding the experimental goals, constraints, and future expansion plans before recommending a solution.

This ensures that customers receive systems that are not only technically capable, but also practical, scalable, and well supported throughout their lifecycle.

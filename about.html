A Brief History

PrecisionTech was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality precision measurement tools to B2B manufacturers, engineers, and quality assurance professionals. Over the past 15 years, we have established ourselves as a trusted leader in the precision instruments industry, serving thousands of clients across manufacturing, aerospace, automotive, and pharmaceutical sectors.

Our journey began with a commitment to excellence and innovation. We recognized a gap in the market for affordable yet accurate precision tools that could meet the demanding requirements of modern industrial applications. Through continuous research, development, and customer feedback, we have built a comprehensive product portfolio that includes digital calipers, micrometers, depth gauges, and specialized calibration equipment. Today, PrecisionTech remains dedicated to delivering products and services that exceed customer expectations and support industrial excellence worldwide.

our vision

Precision, Quality, Innovation

At PrecisionTech, we envision a world where precision measurement is accessible to all manufacturers and engineers, regardless of size or budget. Our mission is to empower industrial professionals with reliable, accurate, and durable precision instruments that enable them to maintain the highest standards of quality in their products and processes.

We believe that precision is not just about accuracy—it's about trust, reliability, and the partnership between supplier and customer. Our team is dedicated to understanding your unique needs and providing solutions that drive your business forward. Through continuous improvement, technological advancement, and unwavering commitment to customer service, we strive to be the preferred partner for precision measurement solutions in the industrial sector.

quality & precision commitment

Every product manufactured by PrecisionTech undergoes rigorous quality control testing. We maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification and adhere to international precision standards to ensure your tools meet the highest industry specifications.

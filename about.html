About HX Technology

HX Technology is a Singapore-based provider of advanced photonics, imaging, and optical analysis solutions for research laboratories, clinical R&D, and technology-driven organizations across the region.

We work closely with researchers, engineers, and institutional partners to deliver carefully selected instruments, complete systems, and practical technical support that enable accurate measurement, reliable imaging, and meaningful scientific results.

Our Focus

HX Technology specializes in solution-oriented technologies including:

  • Advanced microscopy and imaging systems

  • Lasers and light sources for research and industrial applications

  • Spectroscopy and optical analysis tools

  • Detectors, cameras, and time-resolved imaging technologies

  • Precision motion, automation, and system components

Our portfolio combines leading global brands with local expertise to support both standalone instruments and integrated workflows.

Our Approach

We believe that successful projects start with a clear understanding of the application.

Rather than offering generic product lists, HX Technology takes an application-driven approach — working with customers to understand experimental goals, performance requirements, and future scalability before recommending a solution.

This approach allows us to support projects ranging from routine laboratory setups to advanced, multi-technology research platforms.
